title: "Tangent Line"
---

## Definition

The perpendicular (90 degree) line to the [shot line](shot-line). This is the line at which the [cue ball](cue-ball) will travel along after contacting the [object ball](object-ball) IF and ONLY IF the [cue ball](cue-ball) is SLIDING at the point of contact with the [object ball](object-ball)
