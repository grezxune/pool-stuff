## Definition

There are many known aliases for the cue:

- Cue
- Cue stick
- Pool stick
- Stick

Cue is not to be confused with [cue ball](cue-ball).

The anatomy of a cue can be fairly complex and is often made up of multiple independent pieces.

The most common parts of a cue that are talked about are:
- Butt
- Shaft
- [Tip](tip)
