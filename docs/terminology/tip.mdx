## Definition

The tip of a [cue](cue). This is replaceable and come in many different forms - from material to hardness.

Regular playing [cues](cue) are usually outfitted with a softer tip than that of a break cue. Regular playing [cue](cue) tips usually have a hardness that lands in one of these categories:

- Super soft
- Soft
- Medium
- Hard

Every brand of tip will have their own scale for this as well, similar to clothing brands often differing in sizing from one another.

Break [cues](cue) often have a thicker, firmer [shaft](shaft) as well as a much harder tip for driving as much power as possible through the [cue ball](cue-ball).
