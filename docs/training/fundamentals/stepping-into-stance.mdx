## Two steps - that's it!

From [ball address](ball-address), you now need to step into your shot.

### Right handed players

Step with your right foot, followed by a step with your left. Both moving slightly off to the left so your right foot is just to the left of the shot line. Your left food should be off the your left providing stability so you are comfortable and sturdy.

### Left handed players

Step with your left foot, followed by a step with your right. Both moving slightly off to the right so your left foot is just to the right of the shot line. Your right food should be off the your right providing stability so you are comfortable and sturdy.
